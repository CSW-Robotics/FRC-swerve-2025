

Driver:

    Left joystick is for drive
    Right joystick is for angle
    The trigger on the right joystick switches turning to robot reletive, with field reletive turning on the knob on top
    button 3 on the left joystick resets the gyro
    button 12 starts limelight tracking
    

Opperater:

    The i trigger moves the elevator down 
    The right trigger moves the elevator up
    Button 9 (BACK) opens the solonoids like during auto
    The right bumper pushes the coral out
    The left bumper pulls the coral in
    Button 4 (yellow Y) sets the target stage to 3
    Button 3 (red B) sets the target stage to 2
    Button 2 (green A) sets the target stage to 1
    Button 1 (blue X) sets the target stage to the bottem for coral intake

                                        **IMPORTANT**
    The Y, B, X, and A buttons restart the automatic movement of the elevator when you press them

    The BACK button opens the solonoids for 3 seconds you cannot interupt it
